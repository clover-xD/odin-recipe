Yum Yum Sauce

Description

This Japanese steakhouse yum yum sauce, or white sauce, is a mayonnaise-based sauce that's creamy and delicious. Perfect for dipping or drizzling.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or more to taste
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, water, butter, tomato paste, sugar, garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne together until smooth. Cover and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight.
